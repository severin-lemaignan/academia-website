A worldwide pandemic and the Brexit made it: after 6 years in the UK and 3 fantastic years at [Bristol Robotics Lab](https://brl.ac.uk "Bristol Robotics Lab website"), we are moving to Barcelona! and I am super excited to join [PAL Robotics](https://pal-robotics.com "PAL Robotics website") where I'll take the lead on the development of new social capabilities for their service robots.

Cherry on the cake: PAL Robotics has a great community ethos. Expect our cool social tech to be open-source!
