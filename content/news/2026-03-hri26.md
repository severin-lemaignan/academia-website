[HRI'26](https://humanrobotinteraction.org/2026/) is over: excellent organisation in Scotland, non-stop chatting with old & new friends, inspiring talks... one of my best HRI conference!

The [SocialMinds](https://socialminds.iiia.csic.es/) team had a strong presence at the conference, with several papers and presentations, and our cute Emy robot, from the [EMOROBCARE project](https://socialminds.iiia.csic.es/publications/highlight-emorobcare/).

Here are some highlights:

- I presented our alt.HRI paper on the difficulty of build humanoid robots that are also responsible and human-centered. The paper is available [here](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3757279.3788817). You can also download the slides of the presentation from [here](/talks/).

- I participated in a [workshop on Procedural Face generation for robots](https://vizij.ai/events/hri-2026-tutorial/), organised by the great [Semio](https://semio.community/) folks. Download the slides of the presentation from [here](/talks/).
