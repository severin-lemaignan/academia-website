After 4.5 good years at PAL Robotics, I am thrilled to announce that I
have joined the [Institute for Research in Artificial Intelligence
(IIIA)](https://iiia.csic.es) of the Spanish National Centre for Scientific
Research (CSIC) as a Research Scientist in Artificial Social Cognition.

From January 2026, I will co-lead the newly founded SocialMinds AI lab with
Raquel Ros. The lab investigates Artificial Social Cognition with an
interdisciplinary perspective, with colleagues from virtual worlds, sociology,
education sciences, multi-agent systems, and of course, social robotics.

As you can expect, I will primarily focus on social robotics, developing a new
line of research around deep representation for social cognition and social
learning.

Exciting times ahead!
