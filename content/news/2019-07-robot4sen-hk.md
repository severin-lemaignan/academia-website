Inspiring visit in Hong-Kong, where I was invited as external expert to report on the findings of the [Robot4SEN project](http://robot4sen.org/): 70 robots deployed in 30 schools for children with special needs, over a period of 2 years. Fascinating and very humbling to see all these robots being adopted by pupils and teachers, and blended into the daily teaching!

Warm thanks to our hosts, the [HK Vocational Training Council](https://www.vtc.edu.hk/html/en/) for the great organisation.
