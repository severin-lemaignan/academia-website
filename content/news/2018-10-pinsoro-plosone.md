Our paper **The PInSoRo dataset: Supporting the data-driven study of child-child and child-robot social dynamics** presenting the details of the PInSoRo dataset has just been [published in open-access in PLOS ONE](https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0205999). If you are looking for a large (45+ hours) dataset of annotated, naturalistic child-child and child-robot social interaction, have a read, and [download the dataset!](https://freeplay-sandbox.github.io)
