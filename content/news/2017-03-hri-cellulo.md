**Update!** We have been awarded the **Best 'Design' Paper Award** for our paper on the [Cellulo robot](/publications/#ozgur2017cellulo). Great!

We have **two papers accepted for presentation at the [HRI 2017 conference](http://humanrobotinteraction.org/2017/)** next March in Vienna!

The [first one](/publications/#ozgur2017cellulo) presents **Cellulo**, the [new little robots](http://chili.epfl.ch/cellulo) we have created at EPFL: small, cheap, 'child-proof', holonomic and self-localised, they might well be the future of robotics in the classroom! And we already have many ideas how to use them to enhance learning in innovative ways, like this real-world simulation of molecules.

The [second one](/publications/#kennedy2017child) reports on the results of our **experimentations with automatic speech recognition for children**: to put it simply, it does not work yet :-) [Our dataset is online](https://zenodo.org/record/200495).
