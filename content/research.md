{% card(image="/images/hri.svg", side="right") %}
I focus my research on *human-robot interaction*. A few questions I attempt to address: how to let a robot build a **mental models** of the humans it interacts with? What behaviours favor the **long-term acceptance** of the robot? What are the human's **psychological determinants** that influence our relationship with robot? How to **assess the cognitive skills** of our robots?
{% end %}

{% card(youtube="https://www.youtube.com/embed/E_iozVysl5g", side="left") %}
I try to answer these questions in both fundamental and practical ways. I have proposed **[models of how human and robot interact](/publis/lemaignan2014cognitive.pdf)**, I develop **[cognitive architectures for robots](/publis/lemaignan2014human.pdf)**, and I also conduct regular lab and field experiments.
{% end %}

{% card(image="/images/head_pose.svg", side="right") %}
I also design and write **open-source software architectures and tools** that I deploy on different robotic platforms (from Willow Garage's PR2 to Aldebaran's Nao). Those include **[knowledge](https://github.com/severin-lemaignan/minimalkb) [bases](https://github.com/severin-lemaignan/oro-server)**, a **[natural language processor with semantic grounding](https://github.com/severin-lemaignan/dialogs)**, a set of Python tools for **[executive control of robots](https://github.com/severin-lemaignan/pyrobots)**, a **[state-of-the-art simulator](https://github.com/morse-simulator/morse)**.
{% end %}
