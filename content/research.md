I focus my research on **Artificial Cognition for Socially Intelligent Robots**. My
work addresses several fundamental questions like:

- **How can robots build sophisticated mental models** and maintain
**socio-physical situation awareness of their human partners?**

- **What socio-cognitive architectures favor the long-term, responsible
acceptance of robots in human environments?**

By bridging cognitive psychology with robotics and artificial intelligence, I
explore the psychological determinants - such as Theory of Mind - that
influence our relationships with embodied AI.

{% end %}

{% card(image="/images/social-embeddings.png", side="left") %}

My research lays at the **cross-roads of fundamental modeling** in artificial
intelligence (AI) and cognitive sciences, **and experimental research**, with many
"in-the-wild" field experiments, in real-world settings.

I have pioneered work on semantic knowledge representation and recently
introduced the concept of **Social Embeddings**: a promising new avenue for
**modeling dynamic social 'situations' using hybrid LLM/symbolic approaches**.


{% end %}


{% card(youtube="https://www.youtube.com/embed/E_iozVysl5g", side="left") %}

I validate my research through extensive
**real-world deployments**, including lifelong co-design with users in public
spaces like **schools** and **hospitals**.

Check out some of my papers showing how social robots might **help [children
with autism](/publications#lemaignan2022social)** or **[children struggling with
handwriting](/publications#hood2015when)**; how robots might **[help older
adults in geriatric hospitals](/publications#pineda2025socially)** or be trained
to **[assist in a gym](/publications#winkle2020insitu)**.


{% end %}

{% card(video="/images/emotions.mp4", side="right") %}

A core pillar of my work is the creation of **open-source software standards and
tools**. I am the **lead architect and maintainer of
[ROS4HRI](@/softwares.md#ros4hri)** (ROS REP-155), the **first international
software standard for Human-Robot Interaction**. I have developed and deployed
robust architectures across diverse platforms, from Willow Garage’s PR2 to PAL
Robotics' ARI and TIAGo Pro. These tools include the ORO and KnowledgeCore
management modules, as well as perspective-aware Natural Language Processing
frameworks.

{% end %}

{% card(image="/images/safely-tiago.jpg", side="left") %}

**Science should have an impact on society:**
From 2021 to 2025, I have led Technology Transfer at **PAL Robotics** as the
**Head of the AI and Social Robotics group**. In this role, I bridged the gap
between academic research and industry, defining the roadmap for socially
intelligent manipulators and establishing frameworks for **ethics and
responsible robotics**. My research now underpins the backbone architecture of
commercial robots used globally in healthcare, education, and research.

{% end %}
