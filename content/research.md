    <div class="content">
        <p>
        I focus my research on <em>human-robot interaction</em>. A few questions I
        attempt to address: how to let a robot build a <strong>mental models</strong> of the humans it
        interacts with? What behaviours favor the <strong>long-term acceptance</strong>
        of the robot? What are the human's <strong>psychological determinants</strong>
        that influence our relationship with robot? How to <strong>assess the cognitive
            skills</strong> of our robots?
        </p>
    </div>
</article>
